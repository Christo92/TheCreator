Hey you ! Yes I’m talking to you.

The Creator is a pixellated game about being god. Neet, right ?

“Meh, boring to be a god, creating bushes and grass, laaaaaame !”
* goes back to playing LoL or Heartstone *

Hmf. That’s what you must be thinking...
Well you couldn’t be more wrong !

 As if we were to propose a dull game. HELL NO !
In our amazing game, you can incarnate one out of two gods : Haniel or Asmodeus...
Oh trust me, they’re not plain at all ! Just try it yourself !

You could be evil doing or you could be an angel for all we care : do what you want.
As long as you have fun with our work, we’re happy too.


Are you ready ?

So... What's the "story" ?

Well, if you love to be in control, our game is for ya fellow ! * cough *
Okay okay, I’ll be more serious, right fellow ? So…

Long story short, you yan choose between two gods.
Then you start and then BIG BANG ! The world is created for you to play.

At first not many things will be at your disposal and yes, you’ll have to begin with bushes and grass.
I KNOW I KNOW BUT WAIT, DON’T LEAVE ! Fiuuuh, I thought you were gone for a second.
The more you’ll play, the more options will flow and appear for you to use them.
You’re a god, so you can guess that you’ll be able to create many things (trees, explosions, humans…).
and erm… That’s about it, tbh, just discover it by yourself will ya ?

Asmodeus

Are you the evil one ?

Asmodeus is a demon of the Bible with many other names such as Chashmodai, Asmoth, Aesma, Sidonay or Azmonden bla bla bla.

Anyway, you get it, this is the bad guy, the one that takes the evil path. With smoke coming out of his very existence and a dark armor that terrify more than one, he’s born to destroy the world that he hates so much.

Wrongly expelled from heaven for a crime he hadn’t comitted, he wandered through many dimensions, bitter and full of anger and despair.

 Vengeful, he wants everyting to burn and to tear apart everything.

Haniel

Or will you be the kind one

Also known as Anaël, he’s one of the seven angels of the Creation, one of the greatest.

Known for his kindness he seeks peace among every races he encounter and try to spread love and sincerity.

As tiny as a nine years-old kid, he’s wearing a fox mask over his face (presumed inexistant). All we can perceive is his red eyes. Haniel always wears a golden cloak hiding his wings and a hood his halo. He wants tobe seen as a human to get along with them without revealing his identity.
